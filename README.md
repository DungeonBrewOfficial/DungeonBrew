# DungeonBrew

Free tabletop roleplaying resources: steampunk grimdark campaign settings, research-driven supplements, interactive GM tools, and game systems for 5e.

## What's Here

**Dheghom** — A complete campaign setting of industrial ambition and ancient decay. Twenty nations, eight factions, five playable species, original equipment, and monsters.

**Supplements** — Deep-dive supplements covering subjects most games gloss over: horse breeds and economics, gemstone trade, fur trapping, tavern culture, criminal justice, canine companions, and more.

**GM Tools** — Browser-based interactive builders:

- **Quest Builder** — Design quests with structured pattern templates
- **NPC Builder** — Create NPCs with layered motivations and speech profiles
- **Thieves' Guild Builder** — Build criminal organizations from the ground up
- **Ancestors & Ale** — Generate dwarven drinks with full flavor profiles
- **Canine Companions** — Build unique dog companions with stat blocks
- **Hoof & Rein** — Generate horses with breeds, temperaments, and quirks

## Using the Site

Visit [dungeonbrew.github.io/dheghom-site](https://dungeonbrew.github.io/dheghom-site) to browse everything. All content is free to use at your table.
