## Item Descriptions

&emsp; **Barometer, Mercury (25 GP).** A glass and brass device used to measure air pressure. Commonly used for navigation and weather prediction. Grants advantage on Intelligence (Survival) checks made to predict weather.

&emsp; **Clothes, Factory Worker's (3 GP).** Protective clothing designed for industrial work. Includes a thick apron, gloves, steel-toed boots, and a cap or bandana to shield from sparks and grime.

&emsp; **Compass (10 GP).** A small brass compass for navigation. Common among explorers and military officers. Grants +4 on Wisdom (Survival) checks made to avoid becoming lost.

&emsp; **Fire Grenade (25 GP).** A sealed glass flask containing a fire-quenching chemical, with a small charge of gunpowder to disperse it. As a Utilize Action, you can throw it at a point within 20 feet. Upon impact, it bursts, extinguishing all non-magical flames in a 10-foot radius.

&emsp; Although the chemical puts out fire, the resulting reaction creates a toxic cloud in the area for one round. Any creature in the area in the round after a fire has been extinguished must succeed on a Constitution saving throw (DC 13) or take 2d4 poison damage. Furthermore, if the toxic gas is exposed to water, it creates an acid. If a creature is in the gas during damp conditions (such as rain), it will take an additional 2d4 acid damage.

&emsp; **Goggles (10 GP).** Leather-rimmed goggles with thick glass lenses. Protects the eyes from grit, sparks, and flying debris.

&emsp; **Leyden Jar (50 GP).** When you take the Attack action, you can replace one of your attacks with throwing a Leyden Jar. Target one creature or object you can see within 20 feet of yourself. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw (DC 8 plus your Dexterity modifier and Proficiency Bonus) or take 2d6 Lightning damage.

&emsp; **Plague Mask (20 GP).** A fitted leather mask with a long, curved beak filled with dried herbs, straw, and cloth padding to filter the air. Glass eyepieces protect the wearer's vision, and straps secure it tightly around the head. While worn, you have advantage on saving throws against inhaled, non-magical poisons and diseases. In addition, you have disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight due to the narrow field of vision.

&emsp; **Pocket Watch, Brass (50 GP).** A precision-crafted timepiece that provides the exact time, so long as it is wound each day.

&emsp; **Spark-Lighter (3 GP).** A small, spring-loaded metal device that strikes flint against steel to produce a spark. The Spark-Lighter functions as a Tinderbox, but can be used in one hand.

&emsp; **Spring-Hook Launcher (25 GP).** A heavy, hand-cranked device designed to fire a grappling hook with precision. Powered by a wound spring mechanism, it allows for rapid placement of climbing lines. As a Utilize action, you can fire a Grappling Hook connected to a rope up to 50 feet with an attack roll (AC 10 for standard surfaces). The device requires an action to reset after firing.

&emsp; **Thermometer, Mercury (15 GP).** A sealed glass tube filled with mercury, encased in a protective brass frame. Marked with precise temperature measurements, it allows for accurate readings in most conditions. You can determine the exact temperature of your surroundings with an action.
