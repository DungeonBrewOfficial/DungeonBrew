# Equipment

*Firearms, industrial tools, concoctions, and steam-powered technology.*

<small>*Background Illustration by [Elina Lavrienko](https://www.fiverr.com/elina_lavrienko)*</small>

---

The proliferation of black powder has introduced a wide range of firearms, from concealable pistols to military-grade muskets. Concurrently, steam power has been adapted for portable use, resulting in a new class of industrial tools that are powered by back-mounted boilers and often repurposed for combat. Additionally, various alchemical concoctions have been developed as medicines, poisons, and anodynes (snake-oil). The rise of discovery and technological advancement has resulted in a wide range of powerful, dangerous, and deceptive accessories.
