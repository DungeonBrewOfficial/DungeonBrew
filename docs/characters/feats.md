# Feats

Firearms in Dheghom are compatible with certain existing feats at the GM's discretion. For example, the GM may determine that firearms with the Loading Property should benefit from the Crossbow Expert feat in the Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook (pg. 203) by Wizards of the Coast.

## Industrial Combat Training

*General Feat (Prerequisite: Proficiency with Steamcraft Tools; Level 4+)*

You have learned to wield industrial tools in combat, turning heavy machinery into weapons. You gain the following benefits:

&emsp; **Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Intelligence score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

&emsp; **Weaponized Tools.** You gain proficiency with Industrial Tools when using them as weapons (e.g., Pneumatic Rivet Driver, Industrial Rock Drill).

&emsp; **Heavy Strikes.** When attacking with an Industrial Tool, you can add your proficiency bonus to damage instead of attack rolls.

&emsp; **Steam-Hardened.** You have advantage on saving throws against being disarmed while wielding an Industrial Tool in combat.

## Support Specialist

*General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma 13+)*

You gain the following benefits:

&emsp; **Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

&emsp; **Swift Aid.** You can take the Help action as a Bonus Action.

&emsp; **Versatile Assistance.** In lieu of taking the Help action to give Advantage to the next attack roll by one of your allies, you can choose to use it in one of the following ways:

- The next attack roll by one of your allies that hits the target enemy before the beginning of your next turn adds the ability modifier increased by this feat to the attack's damage roll.
- The next attack roll by one of your allies that hits the target enemy before the beginning of your next turn prevents that target from taking Reactions until the end of that ally's next turn.

## Tactician

*General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+)*

You gain the following benefits:

&emsp; **Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

&emsp; **Tactical Repositioning.** When you take damage, you can use your reaction to immediately move 5 feet to an unoccupied space after the damage is dealt. This 5-foot movement does not provoke opportunity attacks.

&emsp; **Strength in Numbers.** As a Bonus Action, you can choose one conscious ally within 5 feet of you. You can choose a new ally to benefit from this feature by using your Bonus Action on a subsequent turn; doing so ends the benefits for the previous ally. A creature can only receive these benefits from one source at a time. As long as you and your chosen ally are conscious and within 5 feet of each other, you gain the following benefits:

- A +1 bonus to Armor Class.
- Advantage on Strength saving throws against any effect that would move you against your will or knock you prone.

## Trained Marksman

*General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, proficiency with martial weapons)*

You have developed specialized expertise in the handling, upkeep, and optimization of firearms. You gain the following benefits:

&emsp; **Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Dexterity or Intelligence score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

&emsp; **Firearm Care.** You gain proficiency with tinker's tools. If you are already proficient with them, you gain Expertise instead.

&emsp; **Steady Aim.** While you are prone and wielding a two-handed firearm, your first ranged attack roll with that firearm on your current turn has Advantage.

&emsp; **Overwatch.** As a Bonus Action, you can choose to provide Overwatch with a loaded firearm you are wielding that does not have the Scatter property. Choose a 5-foot wide, 30-foot long line originating from you. Until the start of your next turn, if a hostile creature moves into or within that area, you can use your Reaction to make one ranged attack with that firearm against that creature. If you hit, the creature's speed is reduced to 0 until the end of its current turn.
