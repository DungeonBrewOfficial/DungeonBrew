# Character Options

<small>*Background Illustration by [Elina Lavrienko](https://www.fiverr.com/elina_lavrienko)*</small>

---

The silver halls of Kizar Korath rise before you, carved deep into the heart of the Brotin Halm Fells, where the dwergar waged a hidden war against shapeshifters in their midst. Or the bells of Bellegrad toll, summoning workers to soot-filled factories while the Plague Wardens silently patrol under the shadow of the Kyrie. Or the necropolis of Killan looms on the Evergreen Isle, its rotting walls barely holding back the dead while the living inside turn against each other. In Dheghom, the choices you make are reflective of a world of ancient grudges, spreading plagues, and shifting allegiances. Every step you take shapes not only your path but the world's fragile balance.
