# DungeonBrew

Steampunk grimdark settings, supplements, and game systems for tabletop roleplaying. Everything on this site is free to use at your table.

### Campaign Settings

<div class="hanging-indent" markdown="1">

**Dheghom.** A world of industrial ambition and ancient decay, where coal-fired empires wage wars over grudges older than memory and the gods have gone silent or mad. Twenty nations, eight factions, and five playable species, each built with the kind of moral complexity that trusts you to make your own judgments. No chosen ones. No clean answers.

<a href="dheghom/" class="md-button md-button--primary">Explore Dheghom</a>

</div>

### Supplements

<div class="hanging-indent" markdown="1">

**System-Agnostic Resources.** Research-heavy supplements covering subjects most games gloss over: realistic horse breeds and economics, gemstone identification and trade, fur trapping and tanning, tavern culture, criminal justice, and more. Several include interactive generators you can use directly in your browser.
