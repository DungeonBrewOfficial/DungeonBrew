# Braenlok's Spawn

<div class="block-row" markdown="1">

<article class="statblock-lore" markdown="1">

Braenlok's flame devours the peace and purpose of the living, leaving a smoldering fire burning within their remains that burns long into death. These undead are manifestations of his agony and rage for the technology. The very sound ignites their wrath as they seek to cleanse the living who would defile the land.

### Husk
A Husk is a mindless, corporeal undead created when a Soot-Wraith's draining attack slays a Humanoid. The victim's body is flash-charred by supernatural heat, leaving behind a brittle, blackened corpse animated by a fragment of the wraith's power. The Husk is a mindless automaton, an empty vessel that moves with a shambling, relentless gait.

Husks serve as the physical extensions of their creators. They are most often found shambling through the burnt-out ruins of villages and forests that have been claimed by Braenlok, often moving in silent packs. They are driven by a simple, overriding need to attack and consume any living thing they encounter.

### Soot-Wraith
A Soot-Wraith is an incorporeal undead formed from the soul of a mortal who has been incinerated by Braenlok's cursed fire. It is an intelligent spirit of sentient ash, corrupted by the need to drain the life from mortals to sustain its own existence. Its touch carries the same supernatural heat that created it, consuming a victim's body from within.

Soot-Wraiths haunt the charred, desolate forests that Braenlok has claimed, often appearing as drifting columns of ash or humanoid shadows in the smoke. They are drawn to the sounds of the living and their warmth.
