<div class="sidebar-content" markdown="1">

The origins of Dheghom are a distant echo, obscured by time and broken by the weight of ten thousand years. Every culture holds its own stories of creation, fragmented and shaped by fading memory, yet few claim to know more than shadows of the truth. Among these, the Aos Sí of Ryhoreiral guard one of the oldest accounts, their long lives preserving pieces of the world that has crumbled around them.  

Their myth, called The Sanctuary, speaks of a time when the gods sought to preserve themselves and their creations. Though many versions of this myth exist, the Aos Sí’s telling has endured as a glimpse into the origins of Dheghom, its hope, its failure, and its descent into ruin.
